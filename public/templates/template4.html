A Heartfelt Welcome

With Joy in Our Hearts

🌺 ✦ 🌺

With the grace of the Almighty and the blessings of our beloved elders, we joyfully announce the wedding of our cherished Sakshi & Saksham.

Two families, one celebration — as these two beautiful souls unite in the sacred bonds of matrimony, we warmly invite you to witness and share in this divine journey. Your love, your laughter and your blessings will make these celebrations infinitely more beautiful.

Come, let us rejoice together with music, vibrant colours, sacred rituals and unbounded joy as we usher in a radiant new chapter of love and togetherness.

"A wedding is not merely the union of two souls —
it is the joyful merging of two families into one."
The Celebrations

Wedding Schedule

Day One
11 June 2026
  • Morning
    🌼 Haldi Ceremony
    A sacred ritual of golden blessings — turmeric paste lovingly applied by family, bestowing a radiant glow and divine protection upon the bride and groom.
    🌼
  • 🎨
    Afternoon
    🎨 Mehendi Celebration
    Intricate henna motifs painted by skilled artists — each pattern a story of love, prosperity and the beautiful life that awaits the couple.
  • Evening
    🎶 Sangeet Night
    An evening ablaze with music, folk melodies and Bollywood joy — both families dancing together in celebration of this glorious union.
    🎶
✦   The Sacred Wedding Day   ✦
Day Two
12 June 2026
  • 🐎
    4:00 PM
    🐎 Baraat Procession
    The groom arrives in a magnificent procession — dhol beats, shehnai melody, dancing relatives and pure elation as he is welcomed at the threshold.
  • 5:00 PM
    🌺 Jaimala
    The bride and groom exchange fragrant marigold garlands — a timeless symbol of joyful acceptance and the beginning of their eternal bond.
    🌺
  • 🔥
    6:00 PM
    🔥 Saat Phere
    Seven sacred vows taken around the holy fire — each phera a solemn promise of love, devotion, prosperity, family and lifelong companionship.
  • 7:00 PM
    ❤️ Sindoor Daan
    The most tender and sacred moment — the groom adorns the bride's hair parting with vermillion sindoor, marking their eternal commitment.
    ❤️
  • 🍽️
    8:00 PM
    🍽️ Wedding Dinner
    A royal feast honouring our cherished guests — traditional Bihari delicacies, lavish spreads and warm Bihari hospitality in full glory.
  • 10:00 PM
    🥹 Vidaai
    The most emotional farewell — as Sakshi bids goodbye to her beloved family, she steps forward into a beautiful new world with Saksham by her side.
    🥹
